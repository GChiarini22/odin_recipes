Arroz con Leche Recipe

An Easy, Delicious Dessert

Arroz con leche (aka rice pudding) is a dish I grew up eating all the time. I grew up in Miami with two immigrant parents. My mother who was Brazilian, and my father who was Argentinian. I had access to all the sugary treats available in the US, like M&Ms, but my mom regularly made arroz con leche and I fondly remember how I would gobble it up until the last rice kernel everytime. It's a classic struggle meal dessert that you can find all over South and Central America.
